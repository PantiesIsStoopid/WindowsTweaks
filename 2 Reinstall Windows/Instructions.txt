Merging ISO with XML
Download Windows 11 ISO:
Windows 11 Download https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/software-download/windows11

Download AnyBurn:
AnyBurn Download  https://www.anyburn.com/download.php

Select the ISO File:

Navigate to and select the Official Windows ISO file you downloaded in Step 2.

Add XML File:

Click on “Add” and select the autounattend.xml file you downloaded in Step 1, or drag it into the AnyBurn window.

Create the New ISO:

Click on “Next,” then “Create Now.” When prompted to overwrite the ISO file, click “Yes.”

Complete the Process:

Once the process is done, close AnyBurn.

Creating a Bootable USB Drive

Prepare Your USB Drive:

Use a USB drive with at least 8 GB of storage.

Download Rufus:

Visit the Rufus website and download the application.  https://rufus.ie/en/

Open Rufus:

Launch Rufus on your computer.

Select Your USB Drive:

In Rufus, choose the USB drive you wish to use.

Select the ISO File:

Click on the option to select your ISO file.

Start the Process:

Click the "Start" button. This process may take some time to complete.
Downloading Your Browser

Access the Shortcut:

Once Rufus has completed, locate and open the shortcut on your desktop.

Download Your Preferred Browser:

Follow the prompts to download the browser of your choice.

Troubleshooting:

If you encounter any errors during the download, restart your computer and try again.
